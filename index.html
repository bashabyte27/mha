About MHA Online Services

Founded in 2003, MHA Online Services is a professional payment facilitation business serving individuals and small businesses across India.

We specialize in managing operational transactions including vendor payments, service settlements, reimbursements, and business-related payouts with complete transparency and compliance.

Our unique service model involves collecting in-hand cash from customers and making direct payments to goods suppliers, grocery vendors, logistics services, and travel providers across different states. Every transaction is backed by proper documentation and follows all applicable financial regulations.
